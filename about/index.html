About me

I’ve been composing music since 10th grade and began with contemporary classical music. I have expanded my field since then but am continually looking for ways to grow as a musician and as a human being. I have experience in songwriting, orchestral and cinematic composition, production, and competency in languages such as Java, Python, C++, and MIPS.

   Because piano was my first instrument, my compositional journey began with contemporary-classical solo piano pieces. Overtime I grew to reach into multi-instrumental music through an orchestral setting and then shortly after a songwriting setting. Finally, getting into production and writing, I hope to use all of these phases as material for whatever I choose to work on next.

Education

  • UC Santa Cruz (Computer Science)
